· 03 / Process Five stages, no shortcuts

How we
work.

i

Consultation

An on-site visit with you, your architect or your designer. We talk material, detail and constraint before anything else.

ii

Quote

A detailed written quote with itemised scope, materials and program. No surprises after signing.

iii

Preparation

Substrates inspected, screeded and waterproofed. The work nobody sees is where the work succeeds or fails.

iv

Installation

On-site daily. Set out drawn before the first tile is cut. Levels, joins and grout lines confirmed in person.

v

Handover

Final clean, sealing, and a walk-through with care notes. We stay reachable long after the invoice clears.

Armadale
Residence.

· Featured Project
Location Armadale, VIC
Scope Exterior Cladding
Material Random Natural Stone
Application Facade & Portico

Random-stone cladding to the home's exterior. Each piece hand-selected, hand-set, mortared on site. The natural variation of colour, scale and form across the wall is the design.

Set against weatherboard and steel. The first surface a visitor reads, and the last one ever replaced.

Random-set stone is among the oldest masonry traditions. Every wall is one of one. Every join carries the hand of the tradesperson who set it.
